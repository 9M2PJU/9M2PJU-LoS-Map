9M2PJU Line-of-Sight Map

This tool helps identify potential line-of-sight paths for UHF (70 cm) and VHF (2 m) amateur radio communications—bands where terrain and obstructions often have a greater impact than atmospheric conditions.

To begin, enter an address or coordinates for both locations. You can then fine-tune the positions by dragging the map markers to see detailed information about the path.

Keep in mind, the results are based on terrain data and provide only an estimate. Real-world variables—like buildings, trees, or other obstructions—may still impact signal quality and communication effectiveness.

  • Open the repeater details page at RepeaterBook, e.g., https://www.repeaterbook.com/repeaters/details.php?state_id=41&ID=365
  • Open your browser's debugger by pressing F12, then go to the Console section.
  • Paste in this command: mymap.getCenter().lat + ',' + mymap.getCenter().lng
  • Your browser may give you a warning about pasting code into the Console,and require that you type allow pasting to continue.
  • You should get back a latitude and longitude string e.g., '44.9491733350656,-122.9332083463669'. Paste that into the transmitting or receiving box below, but be sure to leave off the quote marks.
